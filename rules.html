📋 Game Rules

🌊 How to play

  • 1 Enter your name to start your adventure in protecting the sea.
  • 2 Watch the tutorial to see the ocean full of trash.
  • 3 Create an environmentally friendly to-do list of activities you want to do.
  • 4 Every activity you complete will clean up trash in the ocean.
  • 5 Pressed Button Done after completing activities to clean the sea
  • 6 The more activities we complete, the cleaner our ocean becomes! 🐠
  • 7 You can return to the To-Do List anytime to add or edit activities.
